NYC Spots

Sunset Park

I live near Sunset Park and it's a nice place to play or relax. There is a great view of Manhattan on one side and a public swimming pool for the summer on the other.

It also has a playground, basketball court, and soccer field that you can play in. It's mostly pretty clean and theres a lot of options to choose to have fun.

Subways

The subway has a terrible piss smell in some places and it's very old. I recently went to China for vacation and their subway was many times better than NYC's.

At least the subway does it's job, although not that well since there's quite a bit of delays sometimes.
